Common questions

Clear, cautious, landlord-friendly wording.

Does CMP give legal advice?

No. CMP should guide, organise, flag, and recommend. Legal or possession decisions should still be reviewed by the right professional.

Can CMP pull EPC data?

Yes, the product direction supports EPC register lookup for rating, lodgement date, expiry, certificate number, floor area, and recommendations.

What makes CMP different from a booking site?

CMP records the property state first. The service recommendation comes from what the property actually needs.

Is this useful for multiple properties?

Yes. Portfolio landlords should see which properties are safe, which need attention, and which renewals are coming next.
